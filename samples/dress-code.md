# Dress Code

Our dress code reflects our commitment to modesty, order, and respect for the learning environment.

## General Requirements

- Clothing must be clean, neat, and in good repair
- No clothing with inappropriate graphics, slogans, or logos
- Hair must be clean and well-groomed

## Uniform Standards

| Grade Level | Required Attire |
|-------------|-----------------|
| K–5 | Navy polo, khaki pants or skirt |
| 6–8 | White oxford shirt, navy blazer, khaki pants |
| 9–12 | Full uniform as specified in the student planner |

## Free Dress Days

On designated free dress days, students may wear casual attire that still meets modesty standards. Athletic wear and flip-flops are not permitted.
