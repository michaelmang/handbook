# Attendance Policy

Students are expected to attend all scheduled classes unless excused by a parent or guardian for illness, family emergency, or other legitimate reason.

## Tardiness

Students arriving after the start of homeroom (8:00 AM) are considered tardy. Three tardies in a quarter equal one unexcused absence.

## Absences

Parents must notify the office by 9:00 AM on the day of an absence. Extended absences (three or more consecutive days) require a doctor's note upon return.

## Make-up Work

Students are responsible for obtaining and completing all missed assignments within the number of days absent plus one additional day.
