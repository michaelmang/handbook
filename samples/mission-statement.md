# Mission Statement

We exist to cultivate wisdom and virtue in students through classical Christian education, forming graduates who think clearly, live faithfully, and serve generously.

## Our Vision

To be a community where students encounter the great ideas of Western civilization, learn to reason from first principles, and develop a lifelong love of learning under the lordship of Christ.

## Core Values

1. **Truth** — We pursue knowledge as a reflection of God's ordered creation
2. **Goodness** — We cultivate habits of character that honor God and serve others
3. **Beauty** — We appreciate excellence in art, literature, and all created things
